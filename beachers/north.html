Top 10 most beautiful beaches in the North

This summer you do not need to go too far to the Maldives or Hawaii, in the direction, the countries in this direction also have beautiful paradise islands like dreams known as "raw gems" due to retaining the pristine beauty of man. Now, let's explore the Top 10 beautiful northbound beaches below to schedule an incredibly exciting summer.


1. Livadia Beach, Russia



One of the most popular beaches in the Far East is the beach in Livadia, which owes its name to the neighboring village of the same name, which is located next to Livadia Lake. Like its Crimean namesake, this beach is famous for its clean white sand and cozy enclosed bays, developed infrastructure, beautiful panoramic views, great opportunities for an interesting beach vacation.


2. Shamora Beach, Russia



Shamora is the largest, cleanest and most popular beach near Vladivostok. The name is given in the 70s, Lazurnaya Bay, was not widely spread among the residents of the Primorsky Region. By many indicators, the local beach is among the top ten beaches in Russia. The unique landscape of the Ussuri Bay attracts vacationers not only from the Far East.


3. Triozerie, Russia



Triozerie Bay is located forty kilometers east from Nakhodka, and is famous for its one and a half kilometer beach, covered with white sand, and a crystal clear sea of azure color. The bay got its name due to three freshwater lakes, located in vicinity.


4. Kungasny Cape Beach, Russia



Kungasny Cape Beach in Vladivostok is a vivid example of how from an unattractive place on the coast you can create a beautiful recreation area, impressive cleanliness of the coast and developed infrastructure. It is located almost in the center of the city, in the bukhta Bezymyannaya of Amur Bay, and in recent years, it has become one of the most beloved beach by locals and guests.


5. Chituvai Beach, Russia



Chituvai is located in the city of Nakhodka and is almost always filled with visitors. Musatov Bay, where this beautiful sandy beach is located, is protected on all sides by hills from strong winds, the sea is always calm and warms up perfectly in the summer season. You can get to the beach easily by public transport, and from some areas just by walking. Among disadvantages - not very convenient entrance to the sea, tight parking, and lack of familiar infrastructure as well as garbage left by negligent tourists.


6, Wasaga Beach, Canada



Wasaga is a long beach on the shores of Lake Huron, Toronto, the Province of Ontario. Nearby settlements: Tiny, Clearview and Collingwood. It's considered the most popular among all freshwater resorts in the world. The length of the coastline is 14 km, which offers excellent panoramic views of Georgian Bay and the mountains.


7.Beach Wickaninnish, Canada



Wickaninnish is a popular sandy beach in British Columbia. Easy-accessible and the largest resort in the park, along with others, forms the largest dunes of Vancouver on the west coast.


8. Sunset Beach, Canada



Sunset is a cozy beach at the mouth of the False Creek Bay, next to the famous crossroad of Thurlow Street and Bute Street. This is one of the most popular West End resorts in Vancouver. The coast is visited by tourists from Canada and other countries.


9. Chesterman, Canada



Botanical Beach is a resort with a convenient location in the city of Victoria, British Columbia, Vancouver. It's a place where romantics will enjoy the extraordinary nature of Canada. The largest reserves are located in the nearby territory; there are many clean lakes and rivers with clear water. The landscape is mixed: there are rocky areas, high hills, a part of the shore is sandy.


10. English Bay beach, Canada



English Bay is a beach located in a secluded bay near the West End of British Columbia, in the heart of Vancouver. The resort is known for its picturesque sunset views, clean beach, transparent water and gentle sun.
