Livadia Beach

One of the most popular beaches in the Far East is the beach in Livadia, which owes its name to the neighboring village of the same name, which is located next to Livadia Lake. Like its Crimean namesake, this beach is famous for its clean white sand and cozy enclosed bays, developed infrastructure, beautiful panoramic views, great opportunities for an interesting beach vacation.


Beach description

The beach in Livadia is a many kilometer beach area that combines 4 beach sectors at once, one of which is next to Livadia Lake, and the other 3 are in the area of Anna and Rifovaya bays.


The first beach located on the Livadia Lake is always crowded and is not suitable for secluded vacation lovers. But young people and friendly companies can have fun, because the popular Ibiza club is located nearby. The beach is also suitable for families with children, since the water warms up faster on it than on the others, and in case of bad weather at sea you can swim in the lake. Those who look for secluded and less crowded places can choose the beaches a little further away.

All the beaches in Livadia area are white-sandy, without shells and pebbles, the entry to the water is shallow, with a gradual transition to depth, the bottom is sandy. In addition to the warm sea and clean sand, Livadia's beach captivates with beautiful sea sceneries, views of hills and Far Eastern nature. Plentiful with various species of fish, starfish and other inhabitants of the sea bottom, the underwater world of the coastal zone near the Livadia beach attracts many diving and snorkeling amateurs.

You can get to the beach in Livadia by:

car, following the highway Vladivostok-Khabarovsk;


by regular bus leaving Vladivostok (No. 544) or Nakhodka (No. 22).

When is the best time to go?

The best time to relax on the Far East coast is July and August. At this time, the temperature of water and air reaches maximum levels: about + 20°C on ground and slightly lower in the ocean. Summer in Primorye is rather short and foggy, especially in the southern regions - the humidity there is much higher, which also contributes to a comfortable rest.

Infrastructure

The beach in Livadia is distinguished by good infrastructure, which provides visitors with comfortable vacation. Following facilities are available:

equiped car parking;


rental of beach equipment - umbrellas, deck chairs, sale of rubber rings;


cafe where you can have a meal during the day;


souvenir stores and shops;


toilets and changing rooms.


Lovers of wild outdoor recreation can set a tent right on the beach and live in it for several days. For this purpose there is zone for camping tent equiped on the territory of the beach. Amateurs of a more comfortable vacation have the opportunity to stay at a nearby hotel or at one of 90 bases located near the beach area.

Attractions operating during the summer period - trampolines, slides, water rides and other entertainments - will help diversify a beach holiday not only for children, but also for adults. In a peak season you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also ride bananas, catamarans, ATVs, parachute after a boat.
