Triozerie Bay Beach

Triozerie Bay is located forty kilometers east from Nakhodka, and is famous for its one and a half kilometer beach, covered with white sand, and a crystal clear sea of azure color. The bay got its name due to three freshwater lakes, located in vicinity.


Beach description

Despite the fact that Triozerie is away from large settlements, there are always a lot of people here in the summer season. A variety of recreation centers, private estates and cottages are available for vacationers. You can also stay in campsites and tent camps right on the beach. Among disadvantages there is lack of good roads and unstable cellular communication, which is compensated by the beauty and special atmosphere of these places.


The bay is surrounded by picturesque cliffs, where you can swim in mask and dive great. The water near them is visibly warmer than in open areas, and the underwater world fascinates with its beauty. In the sea you can see a rocky island called Cruiser, where experienced divers usually dive. And hiking lovers can climb the Monument Mountain, where the fantastic views of the bay and the surrounding area open.

The beach is equiped with all the necessary infrastructure - there are toilets, showers, changing cabins, you can do water rides and rent a boat or jet ski. For children, playgrounds, inflatable slides and trampolines are equiped, along the coast there are a variety of cafes, snack bars and stalls.


When is the best time to go?

The best time to relax on the Far East coast is July and August. At this time, the temperature of water and air reaches maximum levels: about + 20°C on ground and slightly lower in the ocean. Summer in Primorye is rather short and foggy, especially in the southern regions - the humidity there is much higher, which also contributes to a comfortable rest.
